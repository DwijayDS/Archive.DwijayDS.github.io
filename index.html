Phoneme Frame Level Classification

Classification and Prediction of Phoneme.

Accomplishments
  • Designed and compared performance of BLSTM, BRNN, RNN and MLP to classify sub samples of speech signals into different (71) phoneme.
  • Achieved anaverage testing accuracy of 79% with MLP after introducing a concept of 23 neighboring frames for each frame.
  • RNN achieved the same accuracy with delay of 13 concept frames in a smaller number of epochs.
  • BLSTM performed the best with highest testing accuracy of 88.7%. Use of GELU activation function in the initial layers seemed to increase the performanceof all models
  • Tools: Python, PyTorch

Human Activity Recognition

Using Dictionary Learning to predict Activity performed by human in videos.

Accomplishments
  • Compared the performance of different algorithms to segment humans out of the video frames.
  • Used STIP algorithm to extract the feature interest points from the segmented frame stack and HOG was used to detect the descriptors.
  • K-SVD algorithm was used to iteratively calculate the sparse matrix and update the dictionaries one activity at a time to minimize L1 norm.
  • Used minimum residual error on testing data and achieved a global accuracy of 83%.
